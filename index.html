Retail Sales Dashboard — Case Study

Problem: Fragmented sales files and inconsistent KPIs. Stakeholders needed a daily-ready dashboard.

Approach: Consolidated sales, returns and inventory using SQL; built a star schema for Power BI; created DAX measures for YoY growth and margin; added slicers for time, region and category.

Synthesized Results:

  • Reduced manual reporting time from ~3 hours to ~10 minutes via automated refresh.
  • Identified top 10% SKUs driving ~45% revenue.
  • Found a ~7% uplift opportunity in promotional mix.

Role: Data cleaning (SQL & Excel), data model & DAX (Power BI), visualization & storytelling.
